Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting of lead singer Dan Reynolds, lead guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman The band first gained exposure with the release of their single "It's Time".

Genres:

Alternative Rap, Electropop, Indie Pop, Pop Rock, Rap Rock, Alternative Rock
